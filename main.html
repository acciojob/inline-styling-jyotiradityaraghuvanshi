Flowers photos

The principal purpose of a flower is the reproduction of the individual and the species. All flowering plants are heterosporous , that is, every individual plant produces two types of spores.
Microspores are produced by meiosis inside anthers and megaspores are produced inside ovules that are within an ovary.

Roses pictures

Petals: The most attractive part of an open flower. Petals can vary in color.
Sepals: Small leaf-like structures on the bud.
Pistil: The innermost part of the flower, which is the female part.
Stamen: The male part of the flower.
Ovary: The swollen part at the base of the pistil, which contains ovules.
